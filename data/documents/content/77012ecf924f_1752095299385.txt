LAREDO, Tex. (KGNS) - A tragic discovery was made early Sunday morning in north Laredo.
The Laredo Police Department has launched a death investigation after the body of a 53-year-old man was found shortly after 7:00 a.m. in the 8000 block of Milo Road.
First responders arrived at the scene and pronounced the man dead.
The case remains under investigation. More details will be released as they become available.
