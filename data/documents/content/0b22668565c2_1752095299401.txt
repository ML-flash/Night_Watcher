ALMA, Ga. (WALB) - WALB is learning more after allegations of theft were made against a Georgia Bureau of Investigation (GBI) agent following the execution of a search warrant in Bacon County.
GBI gave WALB the following statement:
"We are aware of the complaint regarding allegations of theft against a GBI agent. The allegations of theft were made after GBI’s Southeastern Regional Drug Enforcement Office obtained and executed a search warrant at 603 East 7th Street, Alma, Georgia, the property of Jeneco Muurs -El who is also the complainant. At the search warrant, agents seized evidence of suspected drugs, drug-making material, and guns. The criminal investigation remains active. As with any official complaint, the GBI Office of Professional Standards is investigating."
WALB has reached out to the GBI for more information.
