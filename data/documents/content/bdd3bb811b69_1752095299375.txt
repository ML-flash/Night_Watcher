ROLLING FORK, Miss. (WLBT) - The Secretary of State’s office has confirmed to 3 On Your Side an investigation of alleged election fraud over potential vote-buying.
On X, Michael Watson said that he is proud of his team for tracking down “yet another alleged case of election fraud in the state. This case has also been turned over to the Attorney General’s Office for investigation and hopefully prosecution.”
WLBT News has heard from a number of voters about their concerns regarding the latest municipal elections in Rolling Fork and what they tell us was unusual and questionable activity.
