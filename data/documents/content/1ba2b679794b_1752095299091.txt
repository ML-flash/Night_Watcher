Eight nominated to fill two new Hillsborough County judicial positions
The 13th Circuit Judicial Nominating Commission has forwarded the names of eight individuals to the governor to fill two new Hillsborough County Court judgeships created by the enactment of HB 5401.
The nominations include:
- Adam L. Bantner II
- Travis J. Coy
- Branden M. Henderson
- Cheryl Mason
- Colleen O’Brien
- Jessica O’Connor
- Janelle A. Weber
- Alicia Whiting-Bozich
These nominations are the result of the JNC’s deliberations on March 31 following interviews with 20 applicants.
